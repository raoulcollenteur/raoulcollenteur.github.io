Biography

Welcome to my website! Here you will find information on my work, which mostly resolves around groundwater and the development of methods to try and improve our understanding of this important resource. I am passionate about Open Science, Software, and Data, and try to make my work reproducible and enable others to use the methods developed. In my free time you can find me kayaking down a river or hiking up a mountain.
